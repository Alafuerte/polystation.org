Welcome to PolyStation

PolyStation is a space for reimagining relationships beyond traditional norms and configurations, rooted in the principles of non-monogamy, community, and kinship. It is a one-stop social space dedicated to discussing how we connect with others and exploring new ways of building positive and diverse relationships.

About

PolyStation is a movement and community space designed to radically rethink how we relate to one another. Its mission is to challenge the structures of monogamy, individualism, and capitalist-driven isolation by promoting new models of connection rooted in non-monogamy, collective care, and chosen kinship. PolyStation aims to inspire a shift toward more pluralistic, collaborative ways of living and loving, and ultimately to contribute to a more connected, just, and joyful society.
