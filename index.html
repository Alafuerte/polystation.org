What We Do

PolyStation is a one-stop social space dedicated to exploring how we connect with others and finding new ways of building positive and diverse relationship models. We run community events, workshops and provide coaching for people exploring polyamory, and ENM in general. Whether you are new to ethical non‑monogamy, questioning, opening up or simply curious, our inclusive space welcomes people to learn, share and grow.
