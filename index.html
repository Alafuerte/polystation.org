Welcome to PolyStation

PolyStation is a space for reimagining relationships beyond traditional norms and configurations, rooted in principles of non-monogamy, community and kinship. It is a one-stop social space dedicated to discussing how we connect with others and exploring new ways of building positive and diverse relationships.

No experience required! PolyStation is open to everyone of all genders and sexualities; whether you're actively practising non-monogamy, questioning conventional relationships, opening up or simply curious. Come as you are, bring your questions, and be part of an inclusive community that values honesty, agency and connection in all its forms.

About

PolyStation is a movement and community space designed to radically rethink how we relate to one another. Our mission is to challenge the structures of monogamy, individualism, and capitalist-driven isolation by promoting new models of connection rooted in non-monogamy, collective care and chosen kinship. PolyStation aims to inspire a shift toward more pluralistic, collaborative ways of living, loving and ultimately to contribute to a more connected and just society.

Facilitators

Angel Perez is an inclusion specialist and coach with a background in sociology, queer studies, and creative writing. Angel has been practising non-monogamy and supporting others doing the same for many years. Angel offers Ethical Non-Monogamy Coaching (ENM) sessions for individuals and other assemblages. He also runs the queer club night Gayzpacho and is a father of two. Connect with Angel on Instagram via his consulting company Asamper Inclusion or email hello@polystation.org to book a free introductory coaching session with him.

Sion Jones is a community engagement specialist and workshop facilitator with a personal passion for rethinking relationships. Sion encourages open discussion and the sharing of experiences around topics that are still often taboo. He believes that sharing ideas and lived experience helps individuals build confidence and navigate their own journeys. Follow Sion on Instagram.
