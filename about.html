About PolyStation

Rethinking relationships beyond conventional norms.

Our Mission

PolyStation is a movement and community space dedicated to radically rethinking how we relate to one another. Our mission is to challenge the structures of monogamy, individualism and capitalist‑driven isolation by promoting models of connection rooted in ethical non‑monogamy, collective care and chosen kinship. We believe that building positive, diverse relationships can contribute to a more connected and just society.

Our Values

  • Inclusivity: rooted in queer experiece and welcoming all genders, sexualities and relationship styles.
  • Integrity: fostering open communication and trust.
  • Respect: Aknowledging that all experecies and personal stories are valid.
  • Agency: empowering people to define their own connections.
  • Community: building networks of support and shared resources.

Our Facilitators

Angel Perez

Angel is a relational coach, inclusion specialist and founder of PolyStation, creating queer-inclusive spaces to rethink how we relate, care and belong. He has a background in sociology, queer studies, ontological coaching and creative writing. He is also a proud father of two and in his spare time he runs a queer club night called Gayzpacho.

Drawing on lived experience of polyamory, educational training and years of community and inclusion work, Angel has developed a values-based coaching methodology that helps individuals and groups shift their way of relating to others and themselves. In his approach he blends attachment-informed, trauma-aware ontological practice with critical thinking and playful creativity.

Angel offers coaching and workshops for individuals, couples/polycules, families and teams.
