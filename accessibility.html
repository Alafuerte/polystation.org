Accessibility

PolyStation is committed to making our website and community as inclusive and accessible as possible. We want everyone — regardless of ability, device or context — to be able to engage with our content and events.

Measures We Take

  • Using clear headings, labels and navigation.
  • Providing descriptive alternative text for meaningful images.
  • Ensuring sufficient colour contrast for text and backgrounds.
  • Making all content accessible by keyboard.
  • Using semantic HTML to support screen readers.

Limitations

While we aim for a high level of accessibility, there may be some areas of the site or third-party integrations (such as ticketing or mailing list forms) that are not fully accessible. We continue to review and improve these where possible.

Feedback

If you experience barriers using our website, please let us know so we can improve. Email us at hello@polystation.org.
