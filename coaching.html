Coaching & Support

Guidance for your journey into ethical non‑monogamy

Our Coaching Offerings

We provide compassionate, personalised support to help you create and navigate non-monogamous relationships. Choose the format that fits your needs.

Individual Coaching

Personalised sessions exploring your relationship goals and challenges. We help you understand your needs, set healthy boundaries and communicate effectively.

Interpersonal Coaching

Sessions in groups of two or more people who are co-creating relationships together. This could be two or more partners, metamours, friends or chosen family who seek to build understanding, negotiate agreements or foster trust and mutual support.

Co-parenting Coaching

Bringing up children in alternative family arrangements can be difficult, specially when we don't have referents or role models and when all we have learned about parenting is based on monoparental families. Angel can help you navigate the challenges of co-parenting in enviroments where more than two adults are involved in caring for children.

What to Expect

Our sessions focus on creating a non‑judgmental space where you can be honest about your desires and challenges. We use tools such as reflective listening, values exploration and customised exercises to help you thrive in your relationships.

Policies & Confidentiality

Your privacy is important to us. Coaching sessions are confidential and tailored to your comfort level. We respect all identities and relationship structures. We operate on a sliding scale to make coaching accessible. Your ability to pay should not be a barrier to getting support. Contact us to discuss rates and find a plan that works for you.
